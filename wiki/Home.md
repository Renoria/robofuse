## About robofuse

robofuse is a tool for managing and optimizing Real-Debrid downloads. It provides the following main features:

- Processing torrents and finding the best available downloads
- Watching for new torrents added to your Real-Debrid account
- Removing duplicate downloads
- Managing your Real-Debrid account
